FAQ
Are there any limitations during the free trial?

The 30-day free trial enables merchants to try out the Premium plan with all SHOPLINE tools and features, but the shop domain name and ‘3rd party payments and logistics' are excluded.

Can I upgrade to a higher subscription plan anytime? Are there extra charges?

Merchants can upgrade to a higher plan anytime with the remaining time period automatically credited to the higher plan. There is no extra charge for upgrading to a higher plan.

Do I need to input any billing details for the 30-day trial period?

Users do not need to input any billing information before or during the trial period unless you decide to upgrade.

If I want to connect a domain I already own to my SHOPLINE shop, do I have to pay extra?

Connecting a domain you already own to your SHOPLINE shop is free of charge! You may click here to see how to connect your domain to SHOPLINE.
